PERILLA

Perilla is a modern neighbourhood restaurant based in Newington Green. Using humble ingredients of the highest quality, the food focuses on a modern
interpretation of classical European flavours, in an informal and relaxed dining room.

For general enquiries please email info@perilladining.co.uk.
